Hi, I am Elin. I'm currently on the final stretch of my BSc. in Interaction design at Stockholm University, and at the time being I'm an UX intern at SVT Play. I believe that inclusive design is of great importance and that we should acknowledge the fact that technology will be new to all of us at some point. Therefore I find it important to consider potential research behind every experience and make the interaction effortless as well as coherent for the user.

Projects.

Consistency SVT Play

At the time being I have a project about mapping user needs across all SVT Plays platforms, such as web, app and TV. The main idea is to go to the core of user needs by exploring driving forces and needs with our users. My idea is that mapping these core elements will contribute to a more coherent and harmonized experience in our platforms. To do this I am having user tests, sketching user flows and facilitating workshops inhouse. Besides mapping out user needs I am involved in another project concerning initializing a design system at SVT Play. All of this is done alongside working with the double diamond method in an agile manner.

AR Sandbox

As our latest design challenge we decided to investigate how to make an art experience interactive. We were inspired by open source code that used augmented reality to teach topography in the sand for children, this was done by measuring the depths in the sand with an infrared camera and a projector. During a two months process we did contextual inquiries to get an understanding of museums and their visitors, we also had a bodystorming session with users where they tested a lo-fi prototype consisting of yougurt and coloring essence. The final result was an interactive sandbox that allowed you to manipulate the projected colors with your hands, while livestreaming the tempory art on an adjacent wall.
